SCORING RULES (For Round N):

1. STANDARD BID (Bid > 0):
   - SUCCESS (Won == Bid): +20 points per trick.
   - FAILURE (Won != Bid): -10 points for every trick you were over or under.

2. ZERO BID (Bid = 0):
   - SUCCESS (Won == 0): +10 * N points.
   - FAILURE (Won > 0): -10 * N points.

3. BONUSES (Awarded ONLY if Bid is SUCCESSFUL):
   - Capture Number 14: Black (+20), Others (+10).
   - Capture Skull King with Mermaid: +40.
   - Capture Pirate with Skull King: +30.
   - Capture Mermaid with Pirate: +20.

IMPORTANT: If you miss your bid by even one trick, you receive ZERO bonuses and the standard penalty. No bonuses are awarded for destroyed (Kraken) or nullified (White Whale) tricks.
