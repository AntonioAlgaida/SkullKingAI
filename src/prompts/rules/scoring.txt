SCORING RULES — Exact Calculation for Round N:

1. STANDARD BID (Bid ≥ 1):
   SUCCESS (Won == Bid): Score = (+20 × Bid) + Capture Bonuses
   FAILURE (Won ≠ Bid): Score = -10 × |Won - Bid|  (zero bonuses, regardless of captures)

2. ZERO BID (Bid = 0):
   SUCCESS (Won == 0): Score = +10 × N  (where N = current round number)
   FAILURE (Won ≥ 1): Score = -10 × N
   Note: There are NO capture bonuses for zero bids. Only the round multiplier matters.

3. CAPTURE BONUSES (added to successful standard bids only):
   - Black 14 captured: +20
   - Colored 14 (Green/Purple/Yellow) captured: +10
   - Skull King captures each Pirate in the trick: +30 per Pirate
   - Mermaid captures Skull King: +40
   - Pirate captures each Mermaid in the trick: +20 per Mermaid
   Bonuses are LOST entirely if your bid fails. No partial credit.

4. LOOT ALLIANCE BONUS (mutual, end-of-round):
   If a Loot card was played and an alliance was formed (Loot player didn't win the trick):
   → If BOTH the Loot player AND the trick winner each hit their own bid:
   → EACH receives +20 bonus points (total +40 shared between the pair).
   This is separate from capture bonuses and applies on top of everything else.

SCORING EXAMPLES (Round 7 — 7 cards dealt):
- Bid 3, Won 3: +60 base. If you also captured SK + 2 Pirates: +60 + 60 = +120 total.
- Bid 3, Won 4: -10 (missed by 1). Zero bonuses, even if you captured SK.
- Bid 3, Won 2: -10 (missed by 1). Same penalty whether over or under.
- Bid 0, Won 0: +70 (10 × 7). Massive reward in late rounds.
- Bid 0, Won 1: -70 (–10 × 7). Catastrophic in late rounds.

ZERO BID VALUE BY ROUND:
   Round 1:  ±10   Round 6:  ±60
   Round 2:  ±20   Round 7:  ±70
   Round 3:  ±30   Round 8:  ±80
   Round 4:  ±40   Round 9:  ±90
   Round 5:  ±50   Round 10: ±100  ← Most important decision in the game.

CRITICAL SCORING INSIGHTS:
- A swing from "bid 0 success" to "bid 0 failure" in Round 10 = 200 point difference.
- Skull King + 3 Pirates in one trick = +90 bonus. This ALONE can decide a game.
- Winning 1 extra trick on a bid of 2 turns +40 into -10. The difference is -50 points.
- Always calculate: "What do I lose if I miss?" vs "What do I gain if I hit exactly?"
- Bonuses only matter if your bid is correct. Never sacrifice bid accuracy to chase bonuses.
