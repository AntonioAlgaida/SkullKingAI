CARD RANKING — Lowest Power to Highest Power:

TIER 0 — ALWAYS LOSE:
- ESCAPE (5 cards): Always loses to any non-Escape card. Safe dump cards.
- LOOT (2 cards): Always loses the trick BUT creates a cooperation alliance (see LOOT MECHANIC below).
  ⚠ LOOT IS NOT AN ESCAPE. It has a powerful secondary function. Do NOT treat it as just a dump card.
- If ALL cards in a trick are Escapes/Loots, the first card played wins (extremely rare).

TIER 1 — COLORED SUITS (Green, Purple, Yellow — ranks 1-14):
- High card of lead suit wins, unless trumped or beaten by a special.
- All three colored suits are equal in power (Yellow 14 does NOT beat Green 13 unless it is the lead suit).
- Must follow lead suit if you hold any card of that suit.

TIER 2 — BLACK TRUMP (Jolly Roger — ranks 1-14):
- ANY Black card beats ALL colored suits, regardless of value (Black 1 beats Yellow 14).
- Among multiple Black cards: highest value wins.
- Black still loses to all Special characters (Pirates, Skull King, Mermaid).

TIER 3 — SPECIALS (Rock-Paper-Scissors Triangle):
These cards beat ALL numbered suits (including Black). They follow a non-transitive hierarchy:

  PIRATE beats MERMAID beats SKULL KING beats PIRATE.

  Resolution when multiple Specials meet in one trick:
  · If SKULL KING + MERMAID both present → MERMAID wins. (+40 bonus to winner)
  · If SKULL KING present (no Mermaid) → SKULL KING wins. (+30 bonus per Pirate also in trick)
  · If PIRATE present (no Skull King) → FIRST Pirate played wins. (+20 bonus per Mermaid also in trick)
  · If only MERMAID(s) (no Pirate, no SK) → First Mermaid played wins.
  · TIE-BREAKER for identical specials: First played wins (e.g., two Pirates → lead Pirate wins).

TIGRESS — DUAL-ROLE WILDCARD:
- At the moment you play it, you DECLARE its role:
  · Action 61 = Tigress as PIRATE (powerful, will win tricks just like a Pirate).
  · Action 74 = Tigress as ESCAPE (safe, will always lose).
- Choose based on whether you need to win or avoid winning this trick.

DISRUPTORS — Override Everything:
- KRAKEN: Destroys the entire trick. No one wins. No points or bonuses awarded.
  The player who WOULD HAVE won (calculated by treating Kraken as an Escape) leads the next trick.
- WHITE WHALE: Nullifies all Special cards. All cards are resolved by numeric value alone.
  Specials have value 0, so they all lose to any numbered card.
  If ONLY Specials were played (all value 0), the trick is destroyed.
  If both Kraken and White Whale are played in the same trick, the SECOND one played takes effect.

BONUS POINTS (awarded ONLY on a successful bid, NOT on failure):
- Black 14 captured in a won trick: +20 bonus
- Colored 14 (Green/Purple/Yellow) captured: +10 bonus
- Mermaid captures Skull King: +40 bonus to the Mermaid player
- Skull King captures Pirates: +30 bonus PER Pirate captured (e.g., SK + 3 Pirates = +90 bonus!)
- Pirate captures Mermaid: +20 bonus PER Mermaid captured

LOOT ALLIANCE MECHANIC:
- When you play a Loot card AND you do NOT win the trick (someone else wins it):
  → An ALLIANCE is formed between you (the Loot player) and the trick winner.
- At round end, if BOTH allied players successfully hit their bid:
  → EACH player gets +20 BONUS POINTS.
- Strategy: Play Loot into a trick you expect an ally to win. Free +20 for both if you both hit bid.
- ⚠ Loot alliances reward cooperation. Play Loot strategically, not randomly.
