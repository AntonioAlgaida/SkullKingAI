CARD RANKING (Lowest to Highest Power):

1. ESCAPE (White Flag) / LOOT:
   - Always loses (unless all cards are Escapes).
   - Can be played anytime.

2. SUITS (Green/Yellow/Purple):
   - Values 1-14.
   - Standard "High card wins" rules apply. Must follow suit.

3. TRUMP (Black Jolly Roger):
   - Values 1-14.
   - Beats all Green/Yellow/Purple suits.

4. SPECIALS (The "Rock-Paper-Scissors" Logic):
   - These cards BEAT all Suits (1-14) and all Trumps (Black 1-14).
   - Priority Cycle:
     A. PIRATE beats Mermaid.
     B. SKULL KING beats Pirate.
     C. MERMAID beats Skull King.
   - If identical specials collide (e.g., Pirate vs Pirate): First played wins.

DISRUPTORS:
- TIGRESS: Acts as either PIRATE or ESCAPE (Player choice).
- KRAKEN: Destroys the trick. No one wins.
- WHITE WHALE: Nullifies specials. All cards become simple numbers (Specials=0). Highest number wins.
