GAME: Skull King — Exact-Prediction Trick-Taking Card Game.

STRUCTURE:
- 10 Rounds. Round N deals exactly N cards to each player (4 players = 4 cards in Round 4, etc.).
- Each round: BID phase (simultaneous) → PLAYING phase (N tricks) → SCORING.
- Maximum bid is always N (the number of cards dealt).

OBJECTIVE:
Bid the EXACT number of tricks you will win. Winning MORE or FEWER than your bid both lose points.
The game rewards precision, not raw power.

THE DECK (74 unique cards, 75 legal actions):
- COLORED SUITS (4 suits × 14 cards = 56 cards):
  · Green (Parrot), Purple (Map), Yellow (Chest): Ranks 1–14. Equal to each other.
  · Black (Jolly Roger): Ranks 1–14. BLACK IS TRUMP — it beats all colored suits.
- SPECIAL CARDS (18 cards, all have suit=SPECIAL):
  · 5 Pirates: Beat all numbered cards (Green/Purple/Yellow/Black 1-14).
  · 1 Skull King: Beats all Pirates. The most powerful card in the game (with one exception).
  · 2 Mermaids: Beat the Skull King. Lose to all Pirates.
  · 1 Tigress: You choose at play time — act as Pirate (Action 61) OR Escape (Action 74).
  · 5 Escapes (White Flags): Always lose. Safe throw-away cards.
  · 2 Loot: Always lose the trick, but create a COOPERATION ALLIANCE (see scoring).
  · 1 Kraken: DESTROYS the trick. No one wins it. The "would-have-won" player leads next.
  · 1 White Whale: NULLIFIES all specials. All cards resolved purely by numeric value; specials have value 0.

FOLLOW SUIT RULE (Critical):
- If a numbered/trump suit is led, you MUST play a card of that exact suit if you hold one.
- EXCEPTION: You may ALWAYS play any Special card (Pirate, SK, Mermaid, Escape, Loot, Kraken, Whale, Tigress)
  instead of following suit. Specials are free to play at any time.
- If you have no card of the lead suit, you may play ANY card.
- If a Special card is led first, there is NO lead suit — everyone plays freely.

SCORING STAKES:
- Round 10 zero bid: SUCCESS = +100 points, FAILURE = -100 points. Most decisive moment of the game.
- A single Skull King capturing 3 Pirates = +90 BONUS alone, on top of the +20 for winning the trick.
- Missing your bid by just 1 trick costs -10 points AND loses ALL capture bonuses.
- Precision and planning beat raw aggression every round.
